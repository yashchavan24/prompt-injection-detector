How this works: your text is converted into a numeric embedding (capturing overall meaning) and combined with heuristic signals (imperative-word density, roleplay markers, override phrases). A Random Forest classifier trained on labeled injection/benign examples then scores the likelihood of an attack. Scores above 0.7 are blocked, 0.4-0.7 are flagged for review, and below 0.4 are allowed.
